A dated feed of small things I've tried, and what happened. The honest
version — including the ones that didn't work.

### 2026-05-20 — subagents

Tried running two Plan-mode agents in parallel on the same brief. Findings
diverged enough to be useful — kept it as a habit for designs I'm unsure
about.

### 2026-05-12 — hooks

Added a Stop hook that re-runs typecheck after every session. Caught two
issues that would otherwise have been follow-up commits.

### 2026-04-30 — claude.md

Refactored my CLAUDE.md from 80 lines to 24. Quality went up; the agent
stopped second-guessing. Less is more.

### 2026-04-12 — tooling

Switched from manual `grep` to letting the Explore agent do open-ended
searches. Faster, but only if the question is sharp.
